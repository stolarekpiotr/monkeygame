About

This game is based on a memory test that was a part of ongoing research effort aimed at understanding chimpanzee cognition. It's testing short-term memory. You can watch that experiment in a video below:

Rules

  • Memorize positions and numbers in circles
  • Click circles in an ascending order (1->2->3->... and so on)
  • When you click first circle, numbers will hide
  • If you click all circles correctly, you will get to the next level (level equals the amount of circles on board)
  • If the wrong circle is clicked, level will be restarted
  • You can reset the game (back to level 1) with the button
